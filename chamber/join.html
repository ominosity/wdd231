Non-Profit Membership Level

Any non-profits who provide proof of their 501(c)(3) status are eligible for a no-cost membership to the Albany, Oregon Chamber of Commerce. Non-Profit membership includes the following benefits:

  • Inclusion in the Chamber of Commerce directory
  • Mention in the "Non-profits of Albany" section of the annual newsletter
  • Subscription to the monthly newsletter
  • 30% discount on ad space purchased in the "Buy Local" mailer pack
  • Invitation to all Chamber of Commerce events
