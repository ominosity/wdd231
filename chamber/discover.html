Historic Carousel & Museum

Since the early 2000s, this hand-carved carousel has been built, starting with a restored classic 1909 Dentzel Carousel Corporation mechanism. It currently features 41 animals with 52 total planned. The carousel is fun for the whole family, but not the only thing on display. There's also a carousel museum, party rooms, and a gift shop, which sells personally-signed copies of 'Caesar the No drama Llama' (yes, that stamp is a scaled copy of his hoofprint).

503 W 1st Ave, Albany, OR 97321
