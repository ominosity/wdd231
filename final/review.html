Review

The Review Process

While we wish we could give a personal review of all the hikes on our site, we haven't had the opportunity to try all of them. Many come from web searches and are just clarified and summarized here. Your hike reviews allow us to focus only on hikes that are easy, beautiful, and fun.

Review a hike

Please answer as many of the below prompts as you can. Questions prepended with a red are required.

How would you describe the difficulty of the hike?
How would you describe the scenery of the hike?
Would (will) you do this hike again?
